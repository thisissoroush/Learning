## 🎯 What This Book Is About

This is not a book about any specific language, framework, or tool. It is a book about **the mindset, habits, and practices of software craftspeople** — the kind of programmers who consistently produce great work, adapt to change, and take pride in their profession.

> *"Programming is a craft. At its simplest, it comes down to getting a computer to do what you want it to do."*

The word **pragmatic** comes from the Greek *pragmatikos* — "fit for use." A Pragmatic Programmer is one who gets the job done and does it well, adapting their approach to whatever situation they face.
